About Efoy

Efoy detergents and chemicals PLC is one of leading manufacturer and supplier of detergent, cleaning chemical products for Household, Industrial & Institutional. The company is located in Burayu keta, Ethiopia and is part of the Soap, Cleaning Compound, and Toilet products Manufacturing Industry.

company Values

We deliver the at most quality product. We adapt to your changing needs. We use our existing infrastructure and growing network to remain competitive and build strategic partnerships and We offer competitive rates and pricing.

Our Mission

With creativity and innovation we aim to continuously grow our customer base by providing value adding competitively quality products and services, using the most affordable technology and a motivated workforce in safe an environmentally sustainable manner.
